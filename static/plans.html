Common questions

Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel anytime from your Stripe billing portal — no questions asked. You keep access until the end of your billing period.
What counts as one repurpose?
One repurpose = one YouTube URL processed. You get all 4 content types (TikTok hooks, LinkedIn post, Twitter thread, newsletter) from a single repurpose.
Do Quick Tools count separately?
Quick Tools use the same daily limit as regular repurposes on the free plan. Pro users have unlimited access to everything.
Is there a free trial for Pro?
The free plan lets you try the full product with 1 repurpose per day — no time limit. Upgrade to Pro when you need more.
